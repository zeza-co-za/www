# Lead Magnet Risk Register

## Risk: PDF is not uploaded to production

Impact: Visitors submit email but cannot download the guide.
Mitigation: Upload the PDF binary before enabling the form redirect.

## Risk: Form captures email without clear consent

Impact: Privacy and trust risk.
Mitigation: Add consent note and Privacy Policy link near the email field.

## Risk: Unrealistic income interpretation

Impact: Brand and compliance risk.
Mitigation: Keep no-guarantee language visible in guide, page copy and email automation.

## Risk: Affiliate links are not disclosed

Impact: Compliance and trust risk.
Mitigation: Add clear affiliate disclosure anywhere monetised recommendations are used.
