# Content Calendar Template

**Type:** Template

A simple weekly content calendar for planning educational posts, trust-building content, offers, community prompts, and newsletter topics.

## Content pillars

- Educate: teach one useful concept.
- Clarify: simplify a confusing topic.
- Demonstrate: show an example or template.
- Invite: ask people to download, join, book, or reply.

## Weekly cadence

- Monday: beginner education.
- Tuesday: quick tip or AI prompt.
- Wednesday: story, lesson, or mistake to avoid.
- Thursday: resource, template, or checklist.
- Friday: offer, call, or community invitation.

## Quality filter

- Is this useful for a beginner?
- Is the CTA clear?
- Does this avoid misleading income claims?
- Can someone act on this today?
