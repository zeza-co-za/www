# Lead Magnet Content Governance

## Editorial principles

- Be beginner-friendly.
- Use plain language.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid guaranteed-income claims.
- Recommend tools honestly.
- Disclose affiliate relationships clearly.
- Keep privacy and consent visible.

## Update cadence

Review the guide quarterly for:

- Tool changes.
- Platform policy updates.
- SEO guidance changes.
- Analytics setup changes.
- Affiliate disclosure requirements.
- Privacy and data protection obligations.
