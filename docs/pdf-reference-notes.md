# Guide Reference Notes

The Digital Marketing Made Easy Starter Guide references current best-practice areas including:

- Google Search Central guidance for SEO basics, title links, snippets and site discoverability.
- Google Analytics setup guidance for measurement.
- FTC endorsement and disclosure guidance for affiliate/partner recommendations.
- GDPR/European Commission guidance for privacy and data protection considerations where EU users may be targeted.

These references support the compliance-safe positioning of Zeza.co.za as an education and skills platform rather than a guaranteed-income system.
