# AI Prompt Starter Pack

**Type:** Starter Pack

Reusable AI prompts for niche research, content planning, email drafting, offer clarity, productivity, and beginner business workflows.

## Prompt rules

- Give context before asking for output.
- Define the audience, tone, format, and constraints.
- Ask for options before choosing one direction.
- Review AI output for accuracy, ethics, and brand fit.

## Niche prompt

- Act as a beginner-friendly digital marketing coach. Help me compare 5 niche ideas for [audience]. Score each by audience pain, monetisation potential, content availability, trust fit, and ease of starting.

## Content prompt

- Create a 7-day content plan for [niche] that educates beginners, avoids hype, and encourages one clear next step: [CTA]. Include topic, hook, key points, and format.

## Email prompt

- Draft a warm welcome email for someone who downloaded [lead magnet]. Keep it honest, helpful, and beginner-friendly. Include a short disclaimer that results vary.
