# Lead Magnet QA Checklist

Use this checklist before launch.

## Content

- PDF title is correct.
- Zeza.co.za branding is correct.
- No unrealistic income claims.
- Affiliate disclosure language is included where relevant.
- Privacy and consent language is present near the form.

## Technical

- PDF uploaded to `/downloads/digital-marketing-made-easy-starter-guide.pdf`.
- Email capture form stores subscriber details.
- Successful form submission redirects to the guide.
- Welcome email sends the download link.
- Mobile form tested.
- WhatsApp link tested.

## SEO and analytics

- Sitemap updated if a public guide landing page is added.
- Google Search Console configured.
- Analytics configured after privacy approval.
- Conversion event configured for guide downloads.
