## Documentation groups

The sidebar is organised into these sections:

- **Resource Assets** — checklists, planners, templates, prompt packs and downloadable assets.
- **Products and Courses** — course, coaching and digital product definitions.
- **Tools and Platforms** — recommended tool categories and learning-platform notes.
- **Articles and Education** — resource-centre article pages.
- **Community and Skool** — Skool group integration and Lovable handoff.
- **Lead Magnet Operations** — implementation, QA, risk, release and ownership notes.
- **Launch and Automation** — funnel architecture, email copy, social copy and production backlog.
- **Implementation and QA** — routing, PDF checks, references and deployment notes.

## Production rule

Keep the public message simple: **no unrealistic income promises, no pressure tactics, no misleading affiliate claims**. Zeza.co.za should remain practical, transparent and beginner-first.
