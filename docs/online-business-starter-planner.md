# Online Business Starter Planner

**Type:** Planner

A structured planner to clarify your niche, audience, first offer, lead magnet, weekly actions, tools, and launch priorities.

## Business clarity

- Who do you help?
- What problem do you help with?
- What first result can you help them create?
- What free resource introduces your method?

## Launch assets

- One simple homepage.
- One lead magnet.
- One email capture form.
- One welcome email.
- Three educational content pieces.
- One offer page or booking call CTA.

## 30-day focus

- Week 1: clarify audience and offer.
- Week 2: build lead magnet and landing page.
- Week 3: publish content and capture leads.
- Week 4: review metrics and improve.
