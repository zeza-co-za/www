Affiliate Marketing Checklist

A compliance-safe checklist for choosing relevant tools, disclosing affiliate relationships, and recommending products with integrity.

Download assetBook a discovery call

Ethical fit

  • Recommend only tools that match the audience need.
  • Explain who the product is for and who it is not for.
  • Avoid pressure tactics and inflated promises.
  • Keep education and transparency ahead of commission.

Disclosure

  • State when a link may be an affiliate link.
  • Place disclosures near affiliate recommendations.
  • Use plain language.
  • Keep records of partner terms and commission models.

Review criteria

  • Audience fit.
  • Ease of use for beginners.
  • Support and documentation.
  • Price and cancellation clarity.
  • Trust and reputation.
