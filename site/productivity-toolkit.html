Weekly rhythm

  • Plan the week before creating content.
  • Batch similar tasks together.
  • Use AI for drafting, summarising, ideation, and checklists.
  • Keep one measurable weekly outcome.

Focus blocks

  • 30 minutes: idea capture.
  • 60 minutes: content drafting.
  • 60 minutes: page/resource improvement.
  • 30 minutes: review metrics.
  • 30 minutes: follow-up messages.

Automation ideas

  • Welcome email after lead magnet download.
  • Weekly newsletter draft.
  • Content repurposing prompts.
  • FAQ response templates.
  • Lead tracking sheet.
