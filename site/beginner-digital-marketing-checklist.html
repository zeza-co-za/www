Beginner Digital Marketing Checklist

A practical checklist to define your audience, message, channels, offer, content plan, lead magnet, and measurement basics.

Download assetBook a discovery call

1. Foundation

  • Define the outcome you want the website to create.
  • Write one sentence explaining who you help.
  • List the problem or desire your audience already has.
  • Choose one primary offer or lead magnet for launch.

2. Audience

  • Identify the beginner segment you want to serve first.
  • Capture common objections and questions.
  • Write down the words your audience uses to describe their problem.
  • Select one primary channel where this audience already spends attention.

3. Brand and message

  • Create a clear headline.
  • Write a short trust statement.
  • Avoid exaggerated income claims.
  • Define proof points, values, and boundaries.

4. Content and conversion

  • Plan three educational posts.
  • Create one lead magnet CTA.
  • Add a simple email capture form.
  • Track visits, submissions, and downloads.
